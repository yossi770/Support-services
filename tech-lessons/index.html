Patient, Friendly Tech Lessons

Want to video call the grandkids, send photos, or feel safe online? I'll teach you — patiently, one step at a time, in plain language you'll actually remember.

Book a Lesson Call (713) 955-7217
💬 Clear, upfront pricing — always quoted before any lesson

There's no such thing as a silly question here. I teach at your pace, in a calm and friendly way, and I'll never make you feel rushed or embarrassed. By the end, you'll feel genuinely confident.

Popular things to learn

  • Video calling — Zoom, FaceTime, and WhatsApp video, so you can see family whenever you like.
  • Messaging — WhatsApp, texting, and group chats, including sending photos and voice messages.
  • Email — reading, replying, attaching photos, and spotting scams and junk mail.
  • Photos — taking, viewing, organizing, and sharing your pictures (and finding them again).
  • Social media — Facebook and more, safely and without the overwhelm.
  • Staying safe online — recognizing scams, fake calls, and suspicious links with confidence.
  • Everyday apps — maps, weather, banking, streaming, rideshare, and whatever you want to master.

Learning that sticks

I leave you with simple, written notes in your own words, so you can follow the steps again on your own. And if you forget? A quick call or message and I'll happily remind you.

Great for

  • Seniors and anyone who prefers patient, unhurried teaching.
  • Families who want a parent or grandparent confidently connected.
  • Anyone starting fresh with a new phone, tablet, or computer.
