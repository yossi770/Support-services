My approach

I know technology from the inside out

My background is in building and designing software systems. Because I know exactly how these devices and apps are built under the hood, I know how to make them work smoothly for you. I take the frustration out of technology, so you can just enjoy using it.

Most tech help stops at "it works now." I go further. The same care I brought to building reliable, secure software, I bring into your home — setting things up accurately and stably, then explaining them patiently, at eye level, in language that makes sense.

I started Houston Home Tech Help because I kept seeing capable people made to feel "bad with technology," when really the technology was just set up badly. That's the part I fix.

What You Can Expect

Peace of mind, not just a quick fix

Patience, always

I explain things clearly and calmly, as many times as you need — no jargon, no sighing, no making you feel rushed or silly.

Security & accuracy

An architect's habits: careful setups, strong passwords, and sensible privacy — so your devices are safe and dependable.

Honest & upfront

Clear pricing before any work, straight answers, and advice that's in your interest — even when that means "you don't need to buy that."

Here for the long run

I'm not a one-and-done visit. Keep my number — quick remote help is always a call or message away.
