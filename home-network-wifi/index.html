Home Wi-Fi & Printer Setup

Dead zones, dropped connections, a printer that never cooperates? I'll make your home network fast, reliable, and secure — and get every device connected the right way.

Fix My Wi-Fi Call (713) 955-7217
💬 Clear, upfront pricing — always quoted before any work

A home full of devices is only as good as the network behind it. I set up Wi-Fi and connected devices with a software architect's attention to reliability and security — so things stay working, not just working today.

What I can do for you

  • Whole-home Wi-Fi — position or upgrade your router (or add a mesh system) so every room gets a strong, steady signal.
  • Fix slow & dropping internet — track down the real cause of buffering, dead zones, and disconnections.
  • Printers & scanners — wireless printing that actually works from your phone, tablet, and computer.
  • Smart-home devices — cameras, doorbells, speakers, thermostats, and TVs connected and easy to use.
  • Network security — a strong Wi-Fi password, a safer router configuration, and a guest network for visitors.
  • Tidy & labeled — cables organized and passwords written down where you can find them.

No more "why won't it connect?"

I make sure your phones, tablets, computers, and printers all talk to each other properly — and I explain, in plain language, how to reconnect if anything ever drops.

Common problems I solve

  • Wi-Fi that's strong in one room and useless in another.
  • A new printer that won't show up on your computer or phone.
  • Internet that slows to a crawl in the evening.
  • A confusing tangle of devices, apps, and passwords.
